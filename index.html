Minesweeper

This might be the most amount of effort I've put into an assignment so far. left click = check a tile. You die if it is a mine, otherwise it tells you how many mines are around it. Tiles labeled 0 recursively light up tiles around them. right click = flag a tile. You must flag all of the mines correctly (and not flag the non-mine tiles) to win. Other instructions are explained in-game.
